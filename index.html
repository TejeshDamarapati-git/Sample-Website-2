Who We Are

Rooted in Passion, Growing with Purpose

Founded in 2008 by master horticulturist Daniel Harper, GreenScape Pro began as a one-man operation with a pickup truck and a vision: to bring thoughtful, sustainable landscape design to every home in the Pacific Northwest.

Today, we're a team of 35+ certified designers, horticulturists, and craftspeople serving residential and commercial clients across Oregon and Washington. Our philosophy remains unchanged — every landscape should tell a story, nurture its ecosystem, and bring joy for generations.
